Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-10-29

EggRoll is a Chrome extension that provides enhanced functionality for the CCFolia (ccfolia.com) platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 License Information

When you activate your premium license, we collect:

  • License Key: For verification
  • Email Address: For license ownership verification
  • Device ID: A randomly generated UUID to identify your device

The Device ID is a simple random identifier (e.g., "DEV_a1b2c3d4-e5f6-7890-abcd-ef1234567890") stored locally. It does not contain any personal information or hardware specifications.

1.2 Local Settings

Your extension preferences are stored locally on your device:

  • Theme preferences (colors, fonts)
  • Custom macros

This data stays on your device and syncs via Chrome Sync if you have it enabled. We do not have access to this data.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your license information is used to:

  • Verify you have a valid license
  • Enforce the 2-device activation limit
  • Allow you to manage device activations

3. Data Storage

License data is stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore with:

  • HTTPS encryption
  • Authenticated access only
  • Rate limiting

Local settings are stored using Chrome's storage API and never leave your device unless Chrome Sync is enabled.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties.

Your data is only used for license validation and extension functionality.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • View which devices are using your license
  • Deactivate devices to free up activation slots
  • Delete all local data by uninstalling the extension

6. Third-Party Services

This extension uses Firebase (Google) for license storage. Firebase has its own privacy policy at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.
